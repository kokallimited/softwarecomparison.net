Frequently asked questions

Do you rank software by affiliate commission?

No. Most comparison sites are paid to rank products. We don't publish pay-to-rank lists — we give you criteria-based guides and a transparent methodology, and we disclose how we make money.

How do you decide what's "best"?

There's no single best — only what's best for your job, budget and constraints. Our choosing framework scores on fit, total cost of ownership, privacy, integrations, lock-in and support.

Is the site free?

Yes. The guides are free to read. We may earn a commission on some outbound links, clearly disclosed, and it never affects our recommendations.

Why should I trust you over a "Top 10" list?

Because we show our method, declare our incentives, and tell you who should not buy each option. Honesty is the whole point of the site.
